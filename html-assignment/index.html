About Me

name is Natasha I am 19 years old I was born in Ottawa but come from an Italian background due to my mom and also have a citizenship in Italy. When I first meet people, I am shy, but after you get to know me, I am pretty extroverted. I enjoy exploring and learning new things. Curiosity drives me to explore a wide range of topics, including science and technology, as well as arts, sports, and literature.

I am currently a student at AC, I study interactive media design. I chose this degree because I've always had an interest in design and computing. My passion began when my father installed Adobe Suite on our home computer. I asked him if I could play around with Adobe After Effects since as an eleven-year-old, I was intrigued with movies and cinematography and aspired to be a filmmaker. My father agreed, and I had lots of fun playing with it and creating. I had a liking for developing and designing so in high school, ended up becoming the one who would produce the posters throughout the campus and discovered my passion.
